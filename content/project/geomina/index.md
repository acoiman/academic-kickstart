<p style='text-align: justify;'>The main goal of the Mining Geoportal (GeoMiNa) is to serve as a central repository for all data related to mining activities in Venezuela. GeoMiNa is a free and open data resource intended to support public and private management in the mining sector, as well as a means to exchange data between stakeholders.
